Social Media Checklist for College Students :-

CSE0001 – Digital Literacy | Task 4 Part B |PREM PANDEY| 25BET10046

Do's :-
- Always think before you post.
- Always use privacy settings.
- Always post positive, respectful, and constructive messages.
- Always verify information before posting or sharing.
- Always balance social media time with offline activities.

Don’ts
- Do not post your address and phone numbers.
- Do not engage in cyberbullying and negative comments.
- Do not share unverified and fake news.
- Do not over-share your academic and personal problems.
- Do not post anything that may harm your reputation and career.
